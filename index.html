    FAQs

    • Im not getting a slot?
      You need to be quick to login to https://selfregistration.cowin.gov.in and use the pincodes to search for slots.
      Due to thousands of people trying to book just 100 slots there will be a high probablity you won't get it.
      Also try using incognito mode in chrome when booking a slot.
    • Will this book slots automatically?
      No, due to ethical reasons there is no plan to build a feature like this.

    Disclaimer - The telegram channel will only alert you about the availability of slots at the mentioned centres. Availability will change in real time. Book your slots as soon as you get the alert using https://selfregistration.cowin.gov.in
